# OpenClaw Product Guidelines

## Communication Style
- **Senior Engineer Tone:** Maintain a professional and helpful tone, focusing on technical clarity and utility.
- **Concise & Direct:** Be succinct in all communications, avoiding unnecessary conversational filler or preambles.

## UX Principles
- **Minimalist & Local-First:** Prioritize a clean, uncluttered interface and local-first architecture for speed and privacy.
- **Developer-Centric UX:** Focus on powerful CLI tools and extensible developer interfaces.

## Branding & Personality
- **Experimental & Cutting-Edge:** Be bold and innovative with AI, messaging, and real-time voice features.
- **Technical Excellence:** Represent the platform as a sophisticated tool for advanced AI workflows.

## Quality & Documentation
- **Safety & Stability First:** Prioritize high test coverage and robust error handling for a reliable experience.
- **Consistency & Documentation:** Ensure all changes are thoroughly documented and follow established patterns for long-term maintainability.
