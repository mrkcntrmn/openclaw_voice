# Initial Concept
OpenClaw is a personal AI assistant you run on your own devices. It answers you on the channels you already use (WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Google Chat, etc.). The Gateway is the control plane — the product is the assistant.

---

# OpenClaw — Personal AI Assistant

## Target Audience
- **Developers:** Those who want to build, customize, and extend their own AI assistants and tools.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Extensible & Modular:** Easily add new capabilities with custom skills and tools.
- **Real-time Voice API Gateway:** A centralized control plane for real-time voice, talk modes, and audio streaming across various platforms.

## Key Features
- **Multi-channel Integration:** Seamless messaging on Telegram, WhatsApp, Signal, and more.
- **Real-time Voice Gateway:** Support for real-time voice interactions and audio processing.
- **Advanced Capabilities:** Browser control, live Canvas, and agent-driven visual workspaces.
- **Extensible Skills:** Modular platform for bundled, managed, and workspace skills.

## Long-term Vision
- **Open-Source Leader:** To become the definitive open-source platform for personal AI assistants, fostering a vibrant community of contributors and users.
